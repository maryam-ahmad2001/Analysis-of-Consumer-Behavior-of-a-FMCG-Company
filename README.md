# Analysis-of-Consumer-Behavior-of-a-FMCG-Company


The goal of this project was to understand consumer behaviour and suggest immediate marketing strategies. 
We got real-time data for an FMCG company from an analytics firm: Sankhya Analytics, where we were guided by Vinayak Deshpande sir. 
We were not told anything about the company and were just provided with the data. The Company had multiple products and was planning on launching a new product . 
The company worked as B2B , whose main consumers were Channel Partners, or simply put Retailers or the middle men. 
The datasets we were provided with were from 2018 and 2019, i.e. pre-pandemic phase. So we had to keep that in mind while doing this project and suggesting marketing strategies. 

We had 3 Objectives for this project. 

1. The first being understanding the channel partners who responded positively to the launch of a new product and hence to who should be targeted in the new campaign.
2. The second objective was to understand the Consumer Behaviour for both the Channel Partners and the end consumers.
3. The third objective was to develop a scientific method of communication for the said campaign. 

The project was divided into 6 phases. 

1. The first phase was to manage the data where we made the data more useful to us.
2. Then we found out descriptive statistics to understand the data better and created data visualisations. During this phase we completed part of our third objective. 
3. Next we performed predictive modeling using logistic regression, found the significant variables for response and the channel partners who should be focused on; hence completing our first objective. 
4. We performed text mining and created a word cloud using the reviews from the end consumers and finished the second objective. 
5. Then we suggested strategies to the company to better market themselves to the end consumers. 
6. Lastly, we looked at a case study of Country Bean that came into our minds while doing the project.
